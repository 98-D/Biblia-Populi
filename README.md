# Biblia Populi

**Latin for “The Bible of the People.”**

> The Word of God, open to all.

---

## About

**Biblia Populi** is a one-man project — built and maintained by me — born from a simple conviction:

The Holy Scriptures are the true and living Word of God, fulfilled in **Jesus Christ**, crucified and risen.

I believe the Bible testifies to Christ, proclaims the gospel of His death and resurrection, and speaks with enduring authority to every generation.

This platform is not sponsored by an institution, denomination, or publishing house.  
It is a personal labor of faith — an effort to make Scripture freely accessible, clearly presented, and faithful to the text.

---

## Intent

My aim is not to build a brand, but to serve the Word.  
Not to control it, but to make it available.

I believe God’s Word does not belong to gatekeepers — it is given for the world.

**Biblia Populi** exists so that anyone, anywhere, may read, search, study, and share the Scriptures without barrier — and encounter the truth of Jesus Christ.

---

Ancient in name.  
Modern in form.  
Open to all.
