**Biblia Populi**

Latin for **“The Bible of the People.”**

The Word of God, open to all.

Biblia Populi exists to proclaim and preserve the Holy Scriptures as the true and living Word of God — fulfilled in **Jesus Christ**, crucified and risen. We believe the Bible testifies to Christ, reveals the gospel of His death and **resurrection**, and speaks with authority to every generation.

This platform is built on the conviction that God’s Word does not belong to institutions, publishers, or gatekeepers — but is given for the world. Freely accessible. Clearly presented. Faithful to the text.

Designed for reading, searching, studying, and sharing the Scriptures without barrier — so that all may encounter the truth of Jesus Christ.

Ancient in name.
Modern in form.
Open to all.
